About the Data Scientist Role

Data Scientists are analytical experts who utilize their skills in technology and social science to find trends and manage data. They use industry knowledge, contextual understanding, and skepticism of existing assumptions – to uncover solutions to business challenges.

Responsibilities include:

  • Analyzing large amounts of data to discover trends and patterns
  • Building predictive models and machine-learning algorithms
  • Proposing solutions and strategies to business challenges

Ideal candidates should be highly analytical with a knack for analysis, math, and statistics. Critical thinking and problem-solving skills are essential for interpreting data.
